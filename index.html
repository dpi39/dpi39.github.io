i write sometimes

01.

open

she was
a frailty
a naivette
an innocence yet lost

he was
an untied lace
an open space
another letter unpost

02.

minuet for one

she lay oustide on a summer morning
lingering beneath twilight's veil
as the first hints of day
tiptoed through silver curtains
and lit fields of grey
her head tilted toward the sky
she breathed in the night air
while the leaves rustled
ever so softly
as the winds
whispered in her ear
tales of the night
she reached out gently
her fingers outstretched
to touch the skies
and as the daffodils and dandelions danced
in the summer haze,
her thoughts landed softly
upon wisps of cloud

03.

disquiet

a stranger in the dark
down a dim lit road
a simple elegance
kissed by the moon

daggers in
her eyes
a dream reflected

rays that shine soft upon
her face
mellow from light tempest

i pine for the morrow
in time till us meet

she walks into the night
under a shower
of pegasus' tears
stranger to the dark
